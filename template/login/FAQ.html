  • Black and White print One Side
    1. For a single black and white print, page cost will be 1Rs.
    2. As page increase, the cost will be the same.
  • Two sides Black and White print
    1. For a single page of two sides, it will cost you 2Rs.
    2. As page increases, the cost per page could change and it depends on Number of pages in each document
      • If Document has more than 60 pages.Then the cost will be 1 Rs per physical page. eg. your document has 63 pages so it requires 32 physical pages. So cost will be 32 physical pages * 1 Rs per physical page = 32 RS.
      • If Document has more than 40 and less than 60 pages. Then the cost will be 1.5 Rs per physical page.
      • If the document has less than or equal to 40 pages. Then the cost will be 2 Rs per physical page
  • Colour Print
    1. For colour print per page, it will cost 10 Rs.
    2. Increased page number will not affect here.
